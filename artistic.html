The Artistic Headphones

By the middle of 2000 to 2010, the techniques for headphones are got much higher that normal people could not notice the difference for new developed headphones. With this, headphone started to be a part of art. Dr. Dre started this market. He made the series of Dr. Dre headphones. For many people, its design was good enough to promote. People developed the art of headphones with redesigning the headphones.
